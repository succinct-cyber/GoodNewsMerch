FUNDRAISER

There's a message the world needs to hear, and we believe it can travel further than a pulpit.

Goodnews Merch was born out of a simple act of faith. The youth of CAC Goodnews Assembly needed to raise funds to upgrade their church's equipment, so they did what young, creative believers do, they got to work. They designed. They printed. They launched. But somewhere in the process, they discovered something bigger than a fundraiser: a mission.

Every shirt is a conversation.Every print is a quiet but confident declaration that Christ is good news and that good news is worth wearing.

We are not just a merch brand. We are a movement of young Nigerians who refuse to keep their faith confined to four walls. We believe style and scripture can coexist beautifully. That you can be fashionable and faith filled. That what you wear can reflect what you believe, and spark something in someone who needs to hear it.

THE NEED

CAC Goodnews Assembly is a growing congregation of believers in Lagos, Nigeria. To serve better in worship, community, and outreach; they need:
• Sound and multimedia equipment upgrades
• Seating and interior improvements
• Outreach and youth programme funding

Every naira contributed goes directly toward meeting these needs and advancing the work of the church
